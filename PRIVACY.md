# Privacy Policy — ZWSteg Chrome Extension

**Last updated:** March 19, 2026

ZWSteg is a browser extension developed by Tripvento LLC that encodes, decodes, scans, and strips zero-width Unicode characters in text. This privacy policy explains how the extension handles user data.

## Data Collection

ZWSteg does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data. All text processing happens entirely within your browser. No data leaves your device.

## Permissions

The extension requests the following browser permissions:

- **clipboardRead**: Allows you to paste text into the tool for scanning and decoding.
- **clipboardWrite**: Allows you to copy processed output back to your clipboard.

These permissions are used solely for local clipboard interaction. No clipboard data is read, logged, or transmitted anywhere.

## Remote Resources

The extension loads the JetBrains Mono font from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) for UI rendering. No other external resources are loaded. Google's privacy policy for Google Fonts applies to that request: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Analytics and Tracking

ZWSteg does not use analytics, cookies, tracking pixels, or any form of telemetry.

## Third-Party Services

ZWSteg does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services.
